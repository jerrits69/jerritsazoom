Steps on how to book

PICK UP PROCEDURES

1) Choose Your Car

Browse our selection of cars to see which suits you the most.

2) Collect Your Car

We have 2 branches for you to collect your car.

3) Enjoy Your Car

Start driving the car on the road.

RETURN PROCEDURES

1) Park Your Car

Return the car at any of our 3 designated car return point.

2) Car Inspection

AZoom employees will inspect the car for any damages.

3) Payment

AZoom will only deduct the amount from your credit card once all inspections are done.
